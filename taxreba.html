REBATE OF INCOME-TAX IN CASE OF CERTAIN INDIVIDUALS [SECTION 87A]

Section 87A provides rebate of income tax to resident individual, whose total income does not exceed Rs 3,50,000. The amount of rebate is Rs 12,500 or 100% of income tax whichever is less. It is deductible from income tax before calculating health and education cess.
